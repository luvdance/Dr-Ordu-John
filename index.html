Community Outreach

Impactful Outreach in Chokocho Etche LGA

Bringing Healthcare to Underserved Communities

As part of our commitment to holistic care, we recently conducted a vital medical outreach in **Chokocho Etche Local Government Area, Rivers State, Nigeria**. This initiative was specifically designed to bridge the healthcare gap for community members who often face significant barriers to accessing medical services.

  • Provided free comprehensive health check-ups and vital signs monitoring.
  • Offered essential medical consultations for a wide range of common ailments.
  • Distributed crucial medications and health supplies to those in need.
  • Conducted vital health education sessions focused on preventive care practices.

This successful outreach in Chokocho Etche LGA is a testament to our unwavering dedication to expanding access to quality healthcare throughout Rivers State and beyond.
